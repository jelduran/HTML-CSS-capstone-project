Search
Gympoint works in four simple steps.
1- Search

It's simple. On Gympoint homepage, enter the sport activity you are looking for and click search. Altenatively click on the search link on the top menu to view a list of all gyms.

2- Locate

Get to see where the gym is located by clicking on the gym profile, It's even more exciting. You can get the directions from anywhere in the country.

3- Sign up

Sign up to the gym of your choice. Click on the SIGN UP button to get plans and prices and fill the registration form. We also follow up the proccess to make sure you always get the best options.

4- Train

Once you find the gym of your dreams, is time to train hard. Remember you can keep checking out our blog to see new recommendations and offers.
